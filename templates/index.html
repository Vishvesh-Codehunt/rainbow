HOW RAINBOW WILL WORK?

As Rainbow, our aim is to create a quality project that moves slowly and surely instead of being a fast / poor quality project. For example, we will not add large amounts to the liquidity pool at once. We will gradually increase our liquidity pool as our project evaluates and develops over time. We will prove to people how big things can be achieved with small things. This way we will show our quality.

In order to protect both our project and our investors, there will be only 15,000 Circulating Supplies to start with. Over time, our Project will develop new features, and then we will show our main goal to our large community. We will achieve this together with the Rainbow large community.

WARNING

Our Rainbow token project is innovative but has yet to prove itself because it is very recent. Buying, selling and pooling $RAIN can result in large losses as we cannot control the actions of early Mimo DEX traders who have bought large amounts of $RAIN tokens. $ RAINs can only be traded on the Mimo Decentralized Exchange and the price of $RAINs is partially linked to the price of $IOTX. Initial market acceptance was good thanks to the innovative community of IoTeX. We hope the community decides to hold the $RAIN for the long term and slowly add liquidity. The Rainbow token team is keeping a large portion of the $RAIN offering and we intend to use it for the benefit of long term holders. We can only urge you to trust us.

How Can I Buy $RAIN

It's easy and secure process!

01

Install an ioPay Wallet on your phone or desktop and purchase some IoTeX (IOTX)From an IoTeX listed exchange.

02

Send your $IOTX directly to your ioPay Wallet as the destination address. To swap $IOTX for $RAIN, you must manually add $RAIN to your XRC20 Tokens list first.

03

You can then access Mimo.Exchange via Iopay Dapps and purchase Rainbow with the iotex’s you sent to your wallet.
